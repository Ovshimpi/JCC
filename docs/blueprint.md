# **App Name**: JCC Consulting Showcase

## Core Features:

- Resume Writing Showcase: Showcase the resume writing service with details on offerings, process, and sample resumes.
- LinkedIn Building Showcase: Detail the LinkedIn profile building service with before-and-after examples and package options.
- Interview Prep Showcase: Outline the interview preparation service, including mock interviews and feedback sessions.
- Campus to Corporate Services Showcase: Display information on services bridging the gap between academia and professional life.
- Presentation Skills Showcase: Showcase presentation skills training with testimonials and course outlines.
- Email Writing Showcase: Showcase Email Writing skills training with use cases.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Gold (#FFD700) to represent the 'JCC' name and provide a premium feel.
- Background color: Very light, desaturated beige (#F5F5DC). It provides a professional and elegant backdrop.
- Accent color: Dark grey (#4A4A4A) to provide strong contrast and readability against the gold and beige.
- Font pairing: 'Playfair' for headlines (serif, for elegance) and 'PT Sans' for body text (sans-serif, for readability).
- Use professional icons for each service category (e.g., a pen for resume writing, a briefcase for LinkedIn building).
- Clean, grid-based layout with generous white space to emphasize clarity and professionalism.
- Subtle fade-in animations when scrolling to highlight each service section.
